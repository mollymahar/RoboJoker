Welcome to RoboJoker!

Thanks for exploring humor with us! Here's what's going to happen:

  1. We will present you with 20 jokes, five at a time.
  2. For each joke, you'll give us a rating of how good you think the joke is.
  3. We'll feed those ratings into our super-secret algorithm.
  4. Then we'll present you with three new sets of five jokes, and we'll have you rate those, too.

And that's about it!


Show me some jokes!

RoboJoker is a joke recommendation engine, part of a class project for natural language processing at UC Berkeley. Joke text was sourced from Comedy Central (jokes.cc.com), OneLineFun.com, and Reddit.
